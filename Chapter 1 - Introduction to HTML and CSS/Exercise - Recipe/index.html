Lechon Manok

Lechon Manok is a roasted chicken that is coated with marinates such as soy sauce, bay leaves, and garlic. This recipe is best paired with homemade sauce. Lechon Manok became a household favorite by every Filipino. This recipe is usually made for parties and celebrations.

Ingredients for Lechon Manok

  • 2 lbs of Whole Chicken
  • 4 thumbs of crushed ginger
  • 5 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 sliced red onion
  • 1 calamansi
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 1 soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper

Ingredients for Lechon Manok Sauce

  • 1 lb chicken liver
  • 2 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 onion diced
  • 10 tablespoons of white vinegar
  • 8 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 7 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper

Methods for Roasting the Chicken

  • Prepare the chicken and marinate it with soy sauce.
  • Add the crushed garlic, ginger, and sliced red onion and mix it.
  • Add bay leaves inside the chicken.
  • Squeeze a calamansi.
  • Add salt and black pepper. Set it aside to marinate the chicken.
  • Prepare your oven and set it to 450F.
  • Wrap the lechon manok in leaves and wrap the entire thing in aluminum foil.
  • Place the chicken into a roasting pan and place it for 40 minutes.
  • After 40 minutes, take out the chicken.

Methods for Making the Lechon Manok Sauce

  • In the oven, bake the chicken liver in 350F for 15 minutes.
  • After 15 minutes, remove it from the oven and let it cool. Chop the chicken liver to pieces and let it aside.
  • Prepare a cooking pot and pour oil.
  • Add the sliced red onions and crushed garlic.
  • Add the crushed ginger after 2 minutes
  • Add the baked chicken liver and cook it for 3 minutes.
  • Pour the water and let it boil for 2 minutes.
  • Using a hand blender, puree the mixture.
  • Pour the vinegar and let the mixture boil.
  • Stir the mixture and add brown sugar.
  • Add salt and black pepper. Stir and mix it again.
  • Add the bread crumbs while cooking it and whisk it for desired consistency.
  • (Optional): Add the chicken cube and stir it for 3 minutes.
