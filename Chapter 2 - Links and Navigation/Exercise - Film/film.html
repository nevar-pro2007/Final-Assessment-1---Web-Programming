Final Destination

A student named Alex got a premonition about a plane crash on his school trip and saved a few of his classmates. However, after the incident, their situation got complicated as death started to chase them.

Official

Overview of the Film

"Final Destination" is directed by James Wong and co-written by Wong, Glen Morgan, and Jeffrey Reddick. The film stars Devon Sawa, Ali Larter, Kerr Smith, and Tony Todd. It was released in the United States on March 17, 2000, and became a commercial success, grossing approximately $112.9 million against a budget of $23 million.

Plot of the Film

The film centers around Alex Browning, the protagonist, a high school student who experienced a horrifying vision about their plane crashing shortly after take-off. After the flight crew are alerted, he and several other students are pulled out of the flight, which eventually crashes, leaving everyone in the flight killed on board. However, Alex and several other students experienced mysterious deaths as Death reclaims all the lives that are meant to be lost in the accident.
